# Narrative — Alex Chen

Origin chunks. Use at most ONE per email when the conversation calls for context. Each is its own paragraph.

## Why I started Sprig

I left Linear because I kept watching teams ship experimentation tools that didn't actually run experiments — they shipped them, then ran one A/B test a quarter. I wanted to build the version that made it cheap enough to run 40 a year. We hit 12k MAU and $1.4M ARR in 18 months, sold to Replit in 2025.

## Why I'm in motion now

The Replit acquihire wrapped at the 90-day mark — I led the integration handoff myself and then stepped out. I'm not interested in a comfort role. Looking for the next 0→1 problem where the GTM motion is unsolved and the team is small enough that one person can move it.

## What I'm allergic to

Strategy decks without shipping. Big-company process imported into 20-person companies. Anyone who calls themselves a "thought leader." If the work doesn't ship in 2 weeks, I lose interest.

## What I'm actually good at

Onboarding funnels, activation, the messy middle between PMF and scale. Building the first experimentation muscle for a company. Writing the doc that becomes the planning template. I'm bad at: managing managers, optimizing things that already work, enterprise sales.
